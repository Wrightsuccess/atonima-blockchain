Atonima Blockchain White Paper
A high level framework for agent identity, proof of instructions, and agent native transactions.


Atonima Blockchain is a proposed infrastructure layer for the agentic internet. As AI agents become capable of making decisions, initiating payments, accessing services, and communicating with other agents, there needs to be a trusted way to verify identity, origin, permissions, and intent.

Atonima introduces three core primitives.
